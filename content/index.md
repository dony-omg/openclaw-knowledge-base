# OpenClaw Knowledge Base

A practical learning site for OpenClaw, organized for people who want to understand the system, use it with Obsidian, and gradually build more advanced workflows.

## What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is a self-hosted gateway that connects chat channels, AI agents, tools, sessions, memory, and multi-agent workflows.

## Who is this for?

This site is for:
- developers exploring OpenClaw
- people building a personal AI workflow
- users combining OpenClaw with Obsidian and Quartz
- learners who prefer structured notes over scattered docs
